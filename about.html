About ConsoleDNA

Explore the story behind this gaming console history site.

Our Mission

Welcome to ConsoleDNA — a site dedicated to exploring the evolution of gaming consoles that shaped the world of entertainment. Whether you're a lifelong gamer or just curious about the roots of modern gaming, this site is your ultimate guide to the PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo systems.

Why I Created This Site

As a passionate gamer and tech enthusiast, I’ve always been fascinated by how consoles evolved — from 8-bit classics to today’s ultra-realistic systems. I created ConsoleDNA to share that journey and highlight how each brand brought something unique to the gaming world.

What You’ll Find

On this site, you’ll find detailed pages about PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo — each showcasing their key consoles, game-changing features, and contributions to gaming history. From the PS1 to the Switch OLED, it’s all here!

Made By

This website was created as part of a school computing project, where I combined design, research, and coding to build a visually engaging and informative experience. Every section was designed and coded by me, and I hope you enjoy exploring it.

Fun Fact

Did you know? The original GameBoy once survived a bombing during the Gulf War — and it still worked!
